How does it work?

Welcome to our debt recovery website! Here's how it works in easy steps: First, you fill out a form with the details on the money owed to you. Then, using these details, the website makes letter for your claim. This letter is sent to the person who owes you money. You don't need to pay anything upfront. Only when that person pays you back, a fee will be required to cover the website's service. It's simple: fill the form, we send the letter, and only pay when you get your money back.
